# Company Reimbursement Manual Rules

Source manual: `D:\Work\报销\20260520报销\技术服务部厂外报销操作说明.pdf`, extracted 2026-05-20.

Use this reference when checking whether a reimbursement package is complete and whether the reimbursement plan contains the fields needed for OA submission.

## Timing

- After a business trip returns to the company, submit the travel reimbursement process within 3 days.
- Long-term travelers or people with accumulated unreimbursed tickets over 1000 yuan should submit before the 20th of the current month.
- If special circumstances delay submission or small amounts are accumulated, submit by the 20th of the next month at the latest.
- If the time from expense occurrence to the reimbursement form reaching Finance is more than 3 months, it is not reimbursable.
- Reimbursement should be submitted at least once every month.

## OA Form

- Select the corresponding accounting reviewer.
- If there is an electronic invoice, select yes/no correctly.
- For electronic invoices, fill invoice code and invoice number in order; do not swap them.
- Bank account should be the corresponding Minsheng debit card.
- The reason field should follow: client/site + start/end date + total days. For multiple clients/sites, list in chronological order.
- The printed cover must be printed from the OA page on a computer, with the print page complete. Do not print a mobile screenshot as the cover.

## Attachment Ordering And Paper Submission

- Paste attachments in the same order as reimbursement detail rows 1, 2, 3, 4, 5.
- Submit as one bound/pasted booklet through the assistant; do not send loose pages.
- Documents should follow the page-turning order from right to left.
- Tickets should be pasted in order; vehicle tickets are pasted horizontally with the adhesive area on the left.
- For travel reimbursement attachment upload, upload only transportation-ticket images such as bus, train, and flight tickets; merge them into one PDF and rename it clearly.

## Transportation

- Travel transportation includes large transport costs from the company or customer site to bus station, train station, or airport.
- Ride-hailing/taxi reimbursement requires invoice plus itinerary.
- Didi/Gaode and other ride-hailing tools should be checked in advance for invoice availability; rides without invoice may not be reimbursed.
- Taxi machine-printed receipt requires a self-made itinerary table, preferably Excel, printed and attached.
- Taxi type is `交通费`; passenger bus type is `汽车费`.
- If a ticket is lost or ruined due to accident, provide the original payment proof; only 50% of the cost can be reimbursed.
- If departing from or returning directly to home instead of Wuhan, compare against the Wuhan route. If higher than Wuhan, reimburse by Wuhan cost; if lower, reimburse actual cost and explain in remarks.

## Lodging

- Lodging requires invoice, lodging detail bill from hotel front desk, order screenshot, and payment/preorder proof. Missing any one item is non-compliant.
- Lodging should be paid online through Ctrip/Meituan or similar platforms unless there is a special case.
- If staying alone because no one can share the room, add a remark. If sharing, fill in roommate information.
- Standard lodging limit is 200 yuan per person; special circumstances require leader report/approval and a note.
- Prefer issuing special VAT invoice for hotels.
- Paper special VAT invoice: include the deduction copy inside the reimbursement booklet.
- Electronic special VAT invoice: print one extra copy and place it after the whole reimbursement booklet.
- If invoice amount is greater than paid amount, reimburse the actually paid amount.
- If invoice amount is less than paid amount, reimburse the invoice amount.
- Do not round reimbursement amounts.

## Express And Purchases

- Express reimbursement is normally `非差旅`.
- Mailing spare parts/materials uses `修理费`.
- Mailing documents uses `办公费`.
- Special case: mailing flight tickets is travel express cost.
- Buying spare parts/materials requires invoice, order screenshot, and payment screenshot.
- Before reimbursing spare parts/material purchases, send invoices to the assistant. The assistant obtains a warehouse in/out slip, and the reimbursement package must upload the corresponding slip photo.
- Purchases should be reported to the direct leader before buying.

## Substitute Invoices

- When an expense such as cleanroom clothes cannot provide an invoice, traffic invoices may be used as substitute invoices, but the form still chooses `非差旅`.
- Substitute invoice amount must be greater than the actual reimbursed amount.
- Do not write substitute-invoice wording in the reimbursement form remarks. Use a sticky note on the traffic invoice to explain.
- The selected type should match the substitute ticket type. For example, if a traffic ticket is used for tool expenses, choose traffic ticket type.
